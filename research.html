Research

Methods for noisy, hybrid quantum workloads

Our research focuses on optimization and learning under the measurement noise and sampling constraints of near-term quantum systems.

Variational Quantum Algorithms

  • Ansatz design: expressivity vs. trainability trade-offs
  • Noise-aware training and initialization
  • Hardware-efficient, resource-aware circuit families

Quantum Machine Learning

  • Quantum kernels and feature maps
  • Generalization and robustness in finite-shot settings
  • Hybrid quantum–classical model evaluation

Noisy Optimization

  • Gradient-free / stochastic methods for noisy objectives
  • Trust-region and surrogate-assisted strategies
  • Variance reduction and reliable stopping criteria
