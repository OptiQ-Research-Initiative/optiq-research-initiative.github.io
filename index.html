OptiQ Research Initiative

Optimization-first quantum research

We develop methods at the intersection of Quantum Computing, Quantum Machine Learning, and Numerical Optimization, with a practical emphasis on noisy optimization and variational quantum algorithms (VQAs).

Core Topics

  • Variational Quantum Algorithms (VQAs) and trainability
  • Finite-shot / stochastic objectives and robust optimization
  • Quantum machine learning: kernels, feature maps, hybrid models
  • Benchmarking and reproducible evaluation protocols

What we publish

  • Optimization methods and theory for noisy objectives
  • Empirical studies on landscapes, conditioning, and stability
  • Open-source tooling for reproducible experiments

We collaborate with academic and industry partners. If you are interested in joint work, datasets, or benchmarking, please reach out via the contact details listed on the Team page.
